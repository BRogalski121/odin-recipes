Mexican Fried Rice

Description

This recipe is a great substitute for regular egg salad. It has the same flavour you would expect from the smoothness added by the mayonaise, to the tang from the pickles. Due to the texture of the tofu it has a nice firm chew to it. this recipe makes for a great lunch when paired with fresh slices of sour dough bread, or even some crackers.

Ingredients

  • 1 package firm tofu
  • 3 tbsp mayonaise
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 5 pickles (shredded)
  • 1 tbsp prepared mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp chives
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery

Steps

  1. Open tofu package and drain liquid
  2. Pat down tofu with paper towel to remove excess liquid
  3. Crumble tofu into a bowl
  4. Add mayonaise, lemon juice, mustard, garlic and mix
  5. Mix in shredded pickle and chopped garlic
  6. Finely chop chives and set aside
  7. Add salt to taste
  8. Chill tofu egg salad in fridge for 30 min for best flavor
  9. Serve with bread or crackers and garnish with chives
